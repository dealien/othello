</p><h3>ACT V</h3>
<h3>SCENE I. Cyprus. A street.</h3>
<p></p><div>
<i>Enter IAGO and RODERIGO</i>
</div>

</span name="speech1"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.1">Here, stand behind this bulk; straight will he come:</span><br>
</span name="5.1.2">Wear thy good rapier bare, and put it home:</span><br>
</span name="5.1.3">Quick, quick; fear nothing; I'll be at thy elbow:</span><br>
</span name="5.1.4">It makes us, or it mars us; think on that,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.5">And fix most firm thy resolution.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech2"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.6">Be near at hand; I may miscarry in't.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech3"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.7">Here, at thy hand: be bold, and take thy stand.</span><br>
<p><i>Retires</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech4"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.8">I have no great devotion to the deed;</span><br>
</span name="5.1.9">And yet he hath given me satisfying reasons:</span><br>
</span name="5.1.10">'Tis but a man gone. Forth, my sword: he dies.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech5"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.11">I have rubb'd this young quat almost to the sense,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.12">And he grows angry. Now, whether he kill Cassio,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.13">Or Cassio him, or each do kill the other,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.14">Every way makes my gain: live Roderigo,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.15">He calls me to a restitution large</span><br>
</span name="5.1.16">Of gold and jewels that I bobb'd from him,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.17">As gifts to Desdemona;</span><br>
</span name="5.1.18">It must not be: if Cassio do remain,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.19">He hath a daily beauty in his life</span><br>
</span name="5.1.20">That makes me ugly; and, besides, the Moor</span><br>
</span name="5.1.21">May unfold me to him; there stand I in much peril:</span><br>
</span name="5.1.22">No, he must die. But so: I hear him coming.</span><br>
<p><i>Enter CASSIO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech6"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.23">I know his gait, 'tis he.--Villain, thou diest!</span><br>
<p><i>Makes a pass at CASSIO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech7"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.24">That thrust had been mine enemy indeed,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.25">But that my coat is better than thou know'st</span><br>
</span name="5.1.26">I will make proof of thine.</span><br>
<p><i>Draws, and wounds RODERIGO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech8"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.27">O, I am slain!</span><br>
<p><i>IAGO from behind wounds CASSIO in the leg, and exit</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech9"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.28">I am maim'd for ever. Help, ho! murder! murder!</span><br>
<p><i>Falls</i></p>
<p><i>Enter OTHELLO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech10"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.29">The voice of Cassio: Iago keeps his word.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech11"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.30">O, villain that I am!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech12"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.31">It is even so.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech13"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.32">O, help, ho! light! a surgeon!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech14"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.33">'Tis he:--O brave Iago, honest and just,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.34">That hast such noble sense of thy friend's wrong!</span><br>
</span name="5.1.35">Thou teachest me. Minion, your dear lies dead,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.36">And your unblest fate hies: strumpet, I come.</span><br>
</span name="5.1.37">Forth of my heart those charms, thine eyes, are blotted;</span><br>
</span name="5.1.38">Thy bed, lust-stain'd, shall with lust's blood be spotted.</span><br>
<p><i>Exit</i></p>
<p><i>Enter LODOVICO and GRATIANO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech15"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.39">What, ho! no watch? no passage? murder! murder!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech16"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.40">'Tis some mischance; the cry is very direful.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech17"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.41">O, help!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech18"><b>LODOVICO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.42">Hark!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech19"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.43">O wretched villain!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech20"><b>LODOVICO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.44">Two or three groan: it is a heavy night:</span><br>
</span name="5.1.45">These may be counterfeits: let's think't unsafe</span><br>
</span name="5.1.46">To come in to the cry without more help.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech21"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.47">Nobody come? then shall I bleed to death.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech22"><b>LODOVICO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.48">Hark!</span><br>
<p><i>Re-enter IAGO, with a light</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech23"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.49">Here's one comes in his shirt, with light and weapons.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech24"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.50">Who's there? whose noise is this that ones on murder?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech25"><b>LODOVICO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.51">We do not know.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech26"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.52">                  Did not you hear a cry?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech27"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.53">Here, here! for heaven's sake, help me!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech28"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.54">What's the matter?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech29"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.55">This is Othello's ancient, as I take it.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech30"><b>LODOVICO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.56">The same indeed; a very valiant fellow.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech31"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.57">What are you here that cry so grievously?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech32"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.58">Iago? O, I am spoil'd, undone by villains!</span><br>
</span name="5.1.59">Give me some help.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech33"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.60">O me, lieutenant! what villains have done this?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech34"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.61">I think that one of them is hereabout,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.62">And cannot make away.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech35"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.63">O treacherous villains!</span><br>
</span name="5.1.64">What are you there? come in, and give some help.</span><br>
<p><i>To LODOVICO and GRATIANO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech36"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.65">O, help me here!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech37"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.66">That's one of them.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech38"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.67">O murderous slave! O villain!</span><br>
<p><i>Stabs RODERIGO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech39"><b>RODERIGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.68">O damn'd Iago! O inhuman dog!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech40"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.69">Kill men i' the dark!--Where be these bloody thieves?--</span><br>
</span name="5.1.70">How silent is this town!--Ho! murder! murder!--</span><br>
</span name="5.1.71">What may you be? are you of good or evil?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech41"><b>LODOVICO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.72">As you shall prove us, praise us.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech42"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.73">Signior Lodovico?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech43"><b>LODOVICO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.74">He, sir.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech44"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.75">I cry you mercy. Here's Cassio hurt by villains.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech45"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.76">Cassio!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech46"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.77">How is't, brother!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech47"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.78">My leg is cut in two.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech48"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.79">Marry, heaven forbid!</span><br>
</span name="5.1.80">Light, gentlemen; I'll bind it with my shirt.</span><br>
<p><i>Enter BIANCA</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech49"><b>BIANCA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.81">What is the matter, ho? who is't that cried?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech50"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.82">Who is't that cried!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech51"><b>BIANCA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.83">O my dear Cassio! my sweet Cassio! O Cassio,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.84">Cassio, Cassio!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech52"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.85">O notable strumpet! Cassio, may you suspect</span><br>
</span name="5.1.86">Who they should be that have thus many led you?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech53"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.87">No.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech54"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.88">I am to find you thus: I have been to seek you.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech55"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.89">Lend me a garter. So. O, for a chair,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.90">To bear him easily hence!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech56"><b>BIANCA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.91">Alas, he faints! O Cassio, Cassio, Cassio!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech57"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.92">Gentlemen all, I do suspect this trash</span><br>
</span name="5.1.93">To be a party in this injury.</span><br>
</span name="5.1.94">Patience awhile, good Cassio. Come, come;</span><br>
</span name="5.1.95">Lend me a light. Know we this face or no?</span><br>
</span name="5.1.96">Alas my friend and my dear countryman</span><br>
</span name="5.1.97">Roderigo! no:--yes, sure: O heaven! Roderigo.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech58"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.98">What, of Venice?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech59"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.99">Even he, sir; did you know him?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech60"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.100">Know him! ay.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech61"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.101">Signior Gratiano? I cry you gentle pardon;</span><br>
</span name="5.1.102">These bloody accidents must excuse my manners,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.103">That so neglected you.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech62"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.104">I am glad to see you.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech63"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.105">How do you, Cassio? O, a chair, a chair!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech64"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.106">Roderigo!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech65"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.107">He, he 'tis he.</span><br>

</div>

</span name="speech66"><b>GRATIANO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.109">Some good man bear him carefully from hence;</span><br>
</span name="5.1.110">I'll fetch the general's surgeon.</span><br>
<p><i>To BIANCA</i></p>
</span name="5.1.111">For you, mistress,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.112">Save you your labour. He that lies slain</span><br>
</span name="5.1.113">here, Cassio,</span><br>

</div>

</span name="speech67"><b>CASSIO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.1.115">None in the world; nor do I know the man.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech68"><b>IAGO</b></span>
<div>

</span name="5.1.120">Nay, if you stare, we shall hear more anon.</span><br>
</span name="5.1.121">Behold her well; I pray you, look upon her:</span><br>
</span name="5.1.122">Do you see, gentlemen? nay, guiltiness will speak,</span><br>
</span name="5.1.123">Though tongues were out of use.</span><br>
<p><i>Enter EMILIA</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech69"><b>EMILIA</b></span>
<div>

</div>

</span name="speech70"><b>IAGO</b></span>

</div>

</span name="speech71"><b>EMILIA</b></span>
<div>

</div>

</span name="speech72"><b>IAGO</b></span>

</div>

</div>
<h3>SCENE II. A bedchamber in the castle: DESDEMONA in bed asleep;</h3>
<div>
</span name="5.2.1">a light burning.</span><br>
<p><i>Enter OTHELLO</i></p>
</div>

</span name="speech1"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.2">It is the cause, it is the cause, my soul,--</span><br>
</span name="5.2.3">Let me not name it to you, you chaste stars!--</span><br>
</span name="5.2.4">It is the cause. Yet I'll not shed her blood;</span><br>
</span name="5.2.5">Nor scar that whiter skin of hers than snow,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.6">And smooth as monumental alabaster.</span><br>
</span name="5.2.7">Yet she must die, else she'll betray more men.</span><br>
</span name="5.2.8">Put out the light, and then put out the light:</span><br>
</span name="5.2.9">If I quench thee, thou flaming minister,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.10">I can again thy former light restore,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.11">Should I repent me: but once put out thy light,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.12">Thou cunning'st pattern of excelling nature,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.13">I know not where is that Promethean heat</span><br>
</span name="5.2.14">That can thy light relume. When I have pluck'd the rose,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.15">I cannot give it vital growth again.</span><br>
</span name="5.2.16">It must needs wither: I'll smell it on the tree.</span><br>
<p><i>Kissing her</i></p>
</span name="5.2.17">Ah balmy breath, that dost almost persuade</span><br>
</span name="5.2.18">Justice to break her sword! One more, one more.</span><br>
</span name="5.2.19">Be thus when thou art dead, and I will kill thee,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.20">And love thee after. One more, and this the last:</span><br>
</span name="5.2.21">So sweet was ne'er so fatal. I must weep,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.22">But they are cruel tears: this sorrow's heavenly;</span><br>
</span name="5.2.23">It strikes where it doth love. She wakes.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech2"><b>DESDEMONA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.24">Who's there? Othello?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech3"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.25">Ay. Desdemona.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech4"><b>DESDEMONA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.26">Will you come to bed, my lord?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech5"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.27">Have you pray'd to-night, Desdemona?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech6"><b>DESDEMONA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.28">Ay, my lord.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech7"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.29">If you bethink yourself of any crime</span><br>
</span name="5.2.30">Unreconciled as yet to heaven and grace,</span><br>
</span name="5.2.31">Solicit for it straight.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech8"><b>DESDEMONA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.32">Alas, my lord, what do you mean by that?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech9"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.33">Well, do it, and be brief; I will walk by:</span><br>
</span name="5.2.34">I would not kill thy unprepared spirit;</span><br>
</span name="5.2.35">No; heaven forfend! I would not kill thy soul.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech10"><b>DESDEMONA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.36">Talk you of killing?</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech11"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.37">Ay, I do.</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech12"><b>DESDEMONA</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.38">Then heaven</span><br>
</span name="5.2.39">Have mercy on me!</span><br>
</div>

</span name="speech13"><b>OTHELLO</b></span>
<div>
</span name="5.2.40">                  Amen, with all my heart!</span><br>
</div>
